About Tharang 2K25

IHRD Tharang -2K25, the Third National Level Techno-Cultural-Entrepreneurial event organized by the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD), will be held at K K M M P T C Kallettumkara on February 22nd and 23rd, 2025.

This fest will revolve around Artificial Intelligence (AI), emphasizing the responsible use of AI in our daily lives, art and literature, education, industries, and much more.
